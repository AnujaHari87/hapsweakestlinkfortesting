In this study, you will form a group with three other participants. All participants have been recruited over the Prolific platform.
The study consists of two parts and a short survey. The participants you are grouped with will be the same in all parts of this study.

In Part 1, you will have the opportunity to meet with the other members of your group in a virtual meeting. This meeting will last 8 minutes. After Part 1 is finished, we will give you information about Part 2.

Note: As this is a group experiment, and groups proceed at the pace of the slowest group member, on the following pages we have on-screen timers on the top of the pages.

If you don't proceed to the next page or reach a decision in due time, you will be tagged as a dropout and not paid for this study. On instructions pages, the on-screen timers are five minutes and on decision pages, the on-screen timers are three minutes.
