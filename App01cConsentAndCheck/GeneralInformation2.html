We use the currency "Experimental Currency Unit (ECU)" for the study. It will be converted into GBP (£) for payoff at the end of the study. In this study, 10 ECU = £0.20.

The payoff scheme is as follows:

  • If you are not able to proceed with the study during Stage 1, you will not get any payoff and need to return your submission.

  • If you will drop out of the study because we are not able to match you with a team, you will receive a fixed payment of £1.50 to compensate for your time efforts.

  • If you will drop out of the study because one of your team members could not hear or see everyone, you will be exited from the study and receive a fixed payment of £3.00.

  • If you complete the whole study, you will receive a fixed payment of £6.00 for your participation.

  • You may also receive an additional bonus payment in Stage 3. You will receive more detailed information about this before the start of Stage 3.
Note: None of the participants will know how much another participant has been paid.
