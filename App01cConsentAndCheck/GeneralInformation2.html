We use "Experimental Currency Unit (ECU)" for the study. It will be converted into GBP (£) for payoff at the end of the study.
In this study, 10 ECU = £0.20.

If you are not able to proceed with the study during Stage 1, you will not get any payoff and need to return your submission.

You will receive a fixed payment of £6.00 for your participation in the study.

You may also receive an additional bonus payment in Stage 3. You will receive more detailed information before the start of Stage 3.

Note: None of the participants will know how much another participant has been paid.
